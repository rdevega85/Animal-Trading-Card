Sea Otter

Its fur is the densest of any animal on Earth, unlike its fellow marine mammals, it has no blubber to keep it warm.

  • Scientific Name:Lutrinae
  • Conservation Status:Endangered
  • Lifespan:15-20 years
  • Trophic Level:Omniverous

Adult sea otters typically weigh between 14 and 45 kg (31 and 99 lb), making them the heaviest members of the weasel family, but among the smallest marine mammals.
